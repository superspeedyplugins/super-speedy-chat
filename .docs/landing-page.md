### Built for Speed, Not for Shareholders

Most WordPress chat plugins are an afterthought — bloated jQuery monstrosities that drag your page load times into the dirt. Super Speedy Chat is different. It was engineered from the ground up with performance as the top priority:

- **Zero jQuery dependency** — Pure vanilla JavaScript. Lightweight. Fast.
- **Ultra-fast AJAX via mu-plugin** — Bypasses the full WordPress boot process for chat requests. Messages send and receive in milliseconds, not seconds.
- **Minimal footprint** — The entire front-end adds negligible weight to your pages. Your PageSpeed scores stay intact.

### Your Server. Your Data. Your Rules.

With SaaS chat, every message your customers send passes through someone else's infrastructure. Their servers. Their terms of service. Their data retention policies.

With Super Speedy Chat, everything lives on **your own hosting**. Your database. Your backups. Full control, full ownership. No external dependencies, no data leaving your server, no privacy concerns.
